About Me

My name is Abubakar Sadiq Musa, a passionate Geology graduate from Nigeria with a strong foundation in Geosciences, extensive fieldwork experience, and a growing expertise in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning as applied to the Earth sciences.

I aspire to become an educator and advocate for the integration of AI and ML into Geosciences, to make learning more effective, accessible, and impactful.

I proudly served as:

  • Vice President of the Nigerian Mining and Geosciences Society (NMGS) Student Chapter
  • President of the NMGS Student Chapter
  • President of the National Association of Petroleum Explorationists (NAPE) Student Chapter

I also gained significant hands-on fieldwork experience and am currently learning programming languages like Python. I love volunteering and engaging with communities. I am eager to collaborate with individuals and organizations that share my passion for education, innovation, and sustainable development in the geosciences and beyond.
